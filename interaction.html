Product Features

Digital pet that you can take care of by becoming more active, attentive, and interactive with other users; it is responsive to physical activity using an integrated pedometer and OLED display. Interactions and care with the Pep Pet will reward users in-game level experience, food, and other unlocked features. Like any game, this is a way to retain user interest and attention with the device, making them go outside more.

Pairs a parent-facing website where parents can register their email with their kids' Pep Pets. Provides information for parents when their child has made friends or connected with someone to ensure safe interactions.
