Revenue Model

Customers can buy one Pep Pet for $40.00. Raw materials (see below) and labor/assembly cost per unit is $17.04 and $5.00 respectively. The total manufacturing cost is $21.04.

Bill of Materials:

- Raspberry Pi Zero: $5 (1)
- 74HC595 Shift Register: $0.83 (3)
- 10-segment LED Display: $2.25 (3)
- OLED (128x64) Display: $2.78 (1)
- 3D printed Case: $1.80 (1)
- Tacticle Push Button: $0.39 (3)
- 3.7V Lipo Battery: $3.00 (1)
- MPU6040 Accelerometer and Gyroscope: $0.99 (1)

Our product will be sold through all the traditional channels, such as online vendors (our own website, Amazon, etc.), brick and mortar stores, and word of mouth. Yet, we expect most our revenue will be sourced from online sales.

If we compare ourselves to Tamagotchi, Bandai sold...
- 400,000 Tamagotchis in the first year
- 10,000,000 the second year
- 40,000,000 the third year

However we have less brand fame than Bandai so let's say we sell 40,000 units our first year. Only 10% of Tamagotchi's success, and don't blow up as much as them in the following years

40,000 * $40 = $1,600,000 gross our first year. It costs $21 to manufacture 1 Pep Pet, $758,400 net profit first year.
